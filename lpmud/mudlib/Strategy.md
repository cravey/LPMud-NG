# LPmud Progression Strategy Guide

This file has two practical routes for a new player:

1. `Start -> Bank guard kill (class 15 shortsword)`
2. `Start -> Giant kill (class 20 sword)`

All directions start from `room/church` (the church).

---

## Guide 1: Start to Bank Guard

### Goal
- Kill the bank guard in `room/bank`.
- Loot `shortsword` (class 15).

### Core idea
- Do not rush the guard at level 1 with a knife.
- Get free armor and starter weapon.
- Farm safer targets first.
- Upgrade to an orc weapon before trying the guard.

### Step A: Get free starter gear
- From church: `south, east, east, north` -> `room/yard`.
- Pick up and equip:
  - `get knife`
  - `wield knife`
  - `get leather jacket`
  - `wear leather jacket`

### Step B: Build early XP safely
Use nearby low-risk targets before fighting hard-hitters.

- Easy nearby mobs:
  - `beggar` in `room/yard` (level 3, non-aggressive)
  - `harry` in `room/vill_road2` (level 3, non-aggressive)
  - `frog` in `room/plane9` (level 1)

Route to frog from church:
- `south, west, west, west, west, north, north, north, east, east`

If HP drops, retreat to church and recover:
- From village side: go back to `room/church` and wait for regen.

### Step C: Get a better weapon from orcs
You want class 7-9 before trying the guard.

Route to orc valley from church:
- `south, west, west, west, west, west, west`
- This ends at `room/orc_vall`.

Orcs carry random:
- `knife` (class 5)
- `curved knife` (class 7)
- `hand axe` (class 9)

Tactic:
- Pull/fight one at a time when possible.
- If two orcs are on you and HP is dropping fast, retreat east immediately.
- Once an orc dies, loot and equip best drop:
  - `get hand axe` (or best available)
  - `wield hand axe`

### Step D: Advance levels at adventurer guild
Route from church:
- `south, east, east, east, south` -> `room/adv_guild`

Use:
- `cost` (check requirements)
- `advance level` (when XP is sufficient)

Minimum useful target before bank guard:
- Weapon: class 9+
- Armor: leather jacket worn
- HP: full
- Level: at least 2-3 (higher is safer)

### Step E: Kill bank guard
Route from church:
- `south, east, east, south` -> `room/narr_alley`
- `east` -> `room/bank`

Inside bank:
- Guard is level 11 with class 15 weapon.
- Fight at full HP/SP only.
- `kill guard`
- Loot:
  - `get shortsword`
  - `wield shortsword`

## Guide 2: Start to Giant Kill (Class 20 Sword)

### Goal
- Kill one giant in `room/giant_conf`.
- Loot a class 20 sword:
  - `sword of frost`, or
  - `stone cutter sword`, or
  - `sword of fire`

### Core idea
- Giants are a major jump (level 15 monsters, aggressive).
- First secure mid-game gear (bank shortsword) and levels.
- Then grind XP on repeatable mid-tier mobs before attempting giants.

### Step A: Complete Guide 1 first
Required baseline:
- `shortsword` from bank guard (class 15), wielded.
- Leather jacket worn (or better if you find/buy upgrades).
- Comfortable at sustained fights vs level 9-11 mobs.

### Step B: Mid-game XP grind loop
Primary grind zones:
- `room/orc_vall` and `room/fortress` (many orcs, repeatable)
- `room/forest2` troll (level 9)
- `room/bank` guard respawn cycles (if available and manageable)

Safe loop pattern:
1. Fight until ~40-50% HP.
2. Retreat to church.
3. Recover to full.
4. Repeat.

Do not stay in multi-mob rooms when low HP.

### Step C: Level targets before giants
Use `room/adv_guild` regularly with:
- `cost`
- `advance level`

Recommended before first giant attempt:
- Level: around 8-10+ (safer higher)
- Weapon: class 15 equipped
- Full HP/SP at engagement

### Step D: Reach giant area
Route from church to giant lair:
- `south, west, west, west, west, north, north, north, west, west`

This leads through plains to `room/big_tree` and then giant path/lair.

Final approach:
- `room/giant_lair` (contains a giant)
- `west` from lair to `room/giant_conf` (3 giant variants)

### Step E: Giant fight and sword loot
- Pull one target if possible; avoid being hit by multiple giants.
- Focus kill one giant, loot immediately:
  - `get sword of frost` or
  - `get stone cutter sword` or
  - `get sword of fire`
- Equip:
  - `wield sword of frost` (or equivalent)

If fight turns bad:
- Retreat fast and reset.
- Giants are high-risk; do not force low-HP finishes.
